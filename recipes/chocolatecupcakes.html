Chocolate cupcakes

Description

Indulge in the rich and decadent world of chocolate with our Chocolate Cupcakes downloadable recipe.

This recipe is perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings or impressing guests at your next gathering. Made with high-quality cocoa powder and rich dark chocolate, these cupcakes are moist, fluffy, and bursting with intense chocolate flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 125ml Buttermilk
  • 125ml Coffee
  • 105ml Vegetable Oil
  • 140g Plain Flour
  • 185g Caster Sugar
  • 35g Cocoa Powder
  • 35g Choc Chips
  • 1/2 tsp Bicarb
  • 1/4 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sieve all of the dry ingredients
  2. Add in choc chips to your sieved dry ingredients
  3. Mix all the ingredients for a bit together
  4. Put that bowl to the side and grab another bowl for wet ingredients
  5. Add in all of your wet ingredients with eggs
  6. Whisk it until it is completely combined
  7. Now bring your dry bowl and put it in the wet bowl, and mix it
  8. Pour the mixture to your cupcake tins and bake it in 160C for 30min.
